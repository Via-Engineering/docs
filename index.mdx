Via BOM is an AI-powered component search and BOM management platform built for hardware engineers. It reads datasheets end-to-end, answers natural language queries, and carries your project context, design rules, and preferred parts into every search — so you can go from requirement to BOM entry without leaving your workflow.

## Get Up and Running

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Create your Via BOM account or sign in at the app home page. Via loads your account data and available workspaces automatically after login.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick your workspace">
    Use the workspace switcher in the left sidebar to select your organization's workspace. Your active workspace controls which shared projects, libraries, and rulesets are visible to you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run your first search">
    Type a part number, spec, or plain-language requirement — such as `low noise 3.3V LDO` or `100nF 0402 capacitor` — into the search bar. Via returns a ranked shortlist with AI-generated tradeoff explanations for each candidate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a project and build your BOM">
    From the home screen, click **Create Project** to start organizing your selected parts. Add components directly from search results, import an existing BOM file, and export when you're ready to hand off.
  </Step>
</Steps>
